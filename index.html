EVEREST TECHNOLOGY

Connecting home owners and constructors through technology

ABOUT US

A platform for home owners to post their requirements as projects and then the construction professionals can bid on the project as per their preferences, providing control to both parties on who they work with.

HOW IT WORKS

FOR OWNERS

  • Post your construction requirements on app or website

  • Accept the bid based on constructor's profile and the bid amount

FOR CONSTRUCTOR PROFESSIONAL

  • Create your profile with photos of your work and your experience

  • Bid on the construction requirement listing of your choice, once connected with the owner discuss and start the project
